ذكر شرقي منقرض

This book is not about the "Eastern man" we heard about and knew in the past, but rather about the "Eastern masculinity" that has recently infiltrated our society and taken its place—a pale and distorted version. "Eastern masculinity"—in this form and with this behavior—is a very serious illness, not just an illness, but a complete syndrome. It begins with how fathers and mothers raise their sons and daughters and ends tragically, unjustly, for everyone involved. This book will attempt to approach, examine, analyze, and understand the symptoms, types, causes, and complications of "Eastern masculinity," and offer realistic perspectives on changing and treating this intractable syndrome.📖

عظماء الدنيا وعظماء الآخرة

This book is a collection of the author's essays, so you may find some ideas repeated in more than one place. It begins and ends with mystical reflections—"I think the author likes and leans towards them"—starting with a poetic play and ending with "an hour of contemplation" and "moments of intoxication." In between, there are various essays of a religious and political nature, discussing the West and its ambitions, Israel, America, and our position as Arabs regarding our surroundings and our relationship with ourselves.📖
